Rockstar

Summary of Rockstar:

Rock Star is about a young super-successful musician (Ranbir Kapoor) who doesnt believe in love but is advised to fall in love to enrich the expanse of musical experience and become emotionally richer. The musician does allow himself to fall in love but he finds it impossible to fall out of love when asked to do so.
